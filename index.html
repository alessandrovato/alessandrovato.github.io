About the Research

Our focus lies at the intersection of neural signal processing and biocompatible interfaces. By treating the "leaks" in electrode-brain communication as data points rather than just noise, we create more resilient BCI systems.

Active Projects

  • Project Alpha: Neural signal decoding for motor rehabilitation.
  • Project Beta: Reducing electrode impedance via conductive polymers.
  • Project Gamma: Real-time artifact rejection in EEG.
