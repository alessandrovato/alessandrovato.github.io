About the Lab

Our methodology is built on the triad of Respect, Listening, and Adaptation. We treat the brain not just as a signal source, but as a living partner in a digital dialogue. By solving for "interface leaks," we ensure long-term stability in neural implants and non-invasive sensors alike.
