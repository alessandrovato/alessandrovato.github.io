About BCIRehab Lab

Our Mission

At the **BCIReahb Lab**, we are dedicated to restoring human capability through the fusion of neural engineering and clinical rehabilitation. Our work sits at the intersection of neuroscience, robotics, and machine learning.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the brain is not just a signal source, but a living partner in a digital dialogue. Our methodology is built on a triad of:

  • Respect: Prioritizing the biological integrity of the neural system.
  • Listening: Developing higher resolution sensors to capture the brain's intent.
  • Adaptation: Creating BCIs that evolve as the user learns and recovers.

Location & Affiliation

Located within the **Catholic University of America**, we collaborate with clinicians and engineers to move laboratory breakthroughs into real-world rehabilitative settings.
