Current Projects

Exploring the frontier of neural rehabilitation through these active workstreams:

Neural Decoding

Developing high-fidelity algorithms to translate motor cortex signals into digital commands for assistive devices.

Sensory Feedback

Closed-loop systems designed to provide tactile sensations from prosthetic limbs back to the user.

Adaptive BCIs

Machine learning models that adapt to the user's changing neural patterns over long-term use.
