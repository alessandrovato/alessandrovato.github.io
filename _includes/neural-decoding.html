Research

The BCIRehab Lab develops neural engineering technologies for rehabilitation, brain-computer interfaces, and human-machine interaction.

Current Projects

Brain-Computer Interfaces

Developing non-invasive EEG systems for motor rehabilitation and assistive communication.

Neurorehabilitation

Investigating adaptive rehabilitation strategies using neural feedback and wearable sensing systems.

Human-Machine Interaction

Designing intelligent systems that improve interaction between humans and robotic devices.
